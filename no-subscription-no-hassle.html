The hidden cost of subscription alarms

Subscription alarm providers like Verisure and ADT typically charge between £30 and £60 per month, with most contracts locked in for 24 to 36 months. Over a three-year term, that’s often well over £1,500 in monitoring fees alone, on top of the upfront installation cost. And when your contract ends, you don’t own the equipment.

With our installations, you get the same professional-grade Ajax hardware that the subscription companies use, fitted neatly to your property, paid for once. The Ajax app lets you arm, disarm and check your system from anywhere, free, forever.
